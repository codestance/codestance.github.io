Minimalist website for a scientist

Design brief

  • website for a person who just started scientific and teaching career
  • clear, but not dull
  • with picture so that students know what their lecturer looks like
  • with a button redirecting to the appropriate e-mail (there is a lecturer at the university with the exact same name and a lot of e-mail was accidentally sent there)

Design

  • home section with broken, asymetrical layout with overlaping elements to give it a modern look
  • message button in the viewport to get the right email address without scrolling
  • massive headings in the background of teachinng and contact sections, which harmonize well with the content
  • vertically rotated years in the articles section to make it more attractive
  • designed in Adobe XD

Typography

headlines - Frank Ruhl Libres, 700; content - Roboto, 300
